Coverage of UPCAT 🌱

Welcome to your UPCAT Reviewer Hub — a single, quiet home base for every review resource you need, organized by subject and ready whenever you are.

The UPCAT (University of the Philippines College Admission Test) is the entrance exam for hopeful freshman applicants to any UP campus nationwide. It is made up of four official subtests — Language Proficiency, Reading Comprehension, Science, and Mathematics — each given in a single sitting.
