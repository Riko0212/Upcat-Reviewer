Coverage of UPCAT 🌱

Welcome to your UPCAT Reviewer Hub — a single, quiet home base for every review resource you need, organized by subject and ready whenever you are. Think of this page as a clearing in the forest: everything is laid out so you can find what you need quickly and get back to focused practice without the noise of a dozen open browser tabs.

The UPCAT (University of the Philippines College Admission Test) is the entrance exam for hopeful freshman applicants to any UP campus nationwide. It is made up of four official subtests — Language Proficiency, Reading Comprehension, Science, and Mathematics — each given in a single sitting. Your UPCAT result is later combined with your senior high school grades to compute recall under exam pressure.
